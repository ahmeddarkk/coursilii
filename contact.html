Frequently Asked Questions
How do I enroll in a course?
Click "Enroll Now" on any course page. If you have an account, you'll be taken through the enrollment process directly. If not, you'll be prompted to create a free account first.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash on delivery, bank transfer, and payment via WhatsApp. We're working on adding online card payment soon.
Do I get lifetime access to courses?
Yes! Once enrolled, you have lifetime access to all course materials including any future updates we add to the course.
Is there a money-back guarantee?
Absolutely. We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all courses. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 7 days of purchase for a full refund.
What language are the courses in?
Our courses are taught in a mix of Tunisian Arabic (Darija) and French to make them as accessible as possible for Tunisian learners.
