Our Story
Why We Built Coursili

We noticed a gap — talented young Tunisians who wanted to learn high-demand skills like trading, design, and coding, but had no accessible, local, high-quality resource to turn to.

So we built one. Coursili is designed from the ground up to be practical, affordable, and taught in the languages and context that Tunisian learners understand best.

Every course is crafted around real outcomes: getting your first freelance client, making your first trade, building your first website.
